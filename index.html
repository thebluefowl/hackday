DeepSource Hackday

Rules

  1. Participation is mandatory! (Exception: Production went down!)
  2. If you’re in Bangalore, come to office.
  3. You can participate solo/in a team of two.
  4. You have exactly 24 hours to build.
  5. Office will be open through the night. (Yes, we will stock up on Redbull)
  6. If you’re not in Bangalore, you can still participate.
  7. If a majority of your project is built before the hackathon, you maybe disqualified.
  8. There are no themes this time!
  9. Non-engineering teams can collaborate with engineers!

Demos

  1. Your demo should be functional.
  2. You have exactly 10 minutes to show us what you have built.
  3. Winners will be judged by votes.
  4. Demos start at 11am IST on Friday.
